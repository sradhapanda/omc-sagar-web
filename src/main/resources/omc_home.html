    About nERP

    nERP is comprehensive open-source platform which provides an end-to-end solution for a wide range of business applications. It's covering a vast array of business functions, from Manufacturing, Inventory, Accounting, Sales, HR, to eCommerce and more. As a customer, not only does this make nERP easy to use, but it also makes it cost effective since you are choosing only the modules that are needed for your company. It is available in the cloud or on-premise and can be used by businesses of all sizes and software is customizable as per the customer needs. In terms of integration, nERP is API-friendly and integrates easily with other systems seamlessly

    For more details contact us "contact form". We will consult with you and learn your requirements, and then customize a solution that meets your needs.
